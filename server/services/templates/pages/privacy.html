Introduction

Welcome to Smarter Lynx, a link management and URL shortening service operated by Snow Lynx Software, LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you access or use our website, services, and applications (collectively, the "Service").

Smarter Lynx provides content creators with tools to create, manage, and track shortened links, including analytics and health monitoring features to protect affiliate revenue and monitor link performance. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly and transparently. This Privacy Policy should be read in conjunction with our Terms of Service, which govern your use of the Service.

By using Smarter Lynx, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, you must discontinue use of the Service immediately.

Information We Collect

The information we collect depends on how you use our Service. We collect different types of information from Guest Users versus Registered Users (Free and Pro accounts).

Guest Users (No Account)

Privacy First: Guest users can create unlimited links with a 7-day expiration period without creating an account. We do not collect, store, or track any personal information or analytics data for Guest Users. Your links are completely anonymous and automatically expire after 7 days.

Registered Users (Free and Pro Accounts)

For users who create an account, we collect the following types of information:

A. Information You Provide Directly:

  • Email Address: Required for account registration, authentication, and communication
  • Username: A public-facing identifier that may be visible to others when you use custom slugs for your shortened links (e.g., parallax.com/username/custom-slug)
  • Password: Securely hashed using industry-standard one-way hashing algorithms. We never store passwords in plain text and cannot retrieve your original password
  • Payment Information: For Pro account subscriptions, payment card information is collected and processed exclusively by Stripe, our third-party payment processor. We do not store, process, or have access to your payment card details on our servers

B. Information We Collect Automatically:

  • Link Analytics Data: For all shortened links (both registered and guest users), we collect and analyze detailed click event data including: IP-derived country information (via third-party service ip-api.com), browser type and version, device type (desktop/mobile/bot), raw user agent strings, referrer URLs, bot detection flags, and timestamp of each click. This data is stored individually for each redirect to provide comprehensive analytics while maintaining an aggregate click counter for quick access.
  • IP Addresses: We collect IP addresses for security purposes, fraud prevention, and to derive geographic location (country) for analytics. IP addresses are sent to ip-api.com, a free third-party geolocation service, to determine the country of origin. If this service is unavailable or rate-limited, geographic data will be recorded as unknown. We do not store raw IP addresses in our click tracking data.
  • Browser and Device Information: Information about your browser type, operating system, device type, and screen resolution
  • Authentication Cookies: We use secure, HTTPS-only cookies to store a JSON Web Token (JWT) containing only your user ID for authentication and session management. This cookie is verified on every request to maintain your logged-in state
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Service, including pages visited, features used, links created and modified, and time spent on the platform

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect from registered users for the following purposes:

  1. Provide and Maintain the Service: To create and manage your account, enable link creation and management, and provide access to Service features based on your account tier (Free or Pro)
  2. Authentication and Security: To authenticate your identity using JWT cookies, verify your user ID on every request, prevent unauthorized access, and protect against fraud and abuse
  3. Process Payments: To facilitate payment processing through Stripe for Pro account subscriptions, manage billing cycles, and handle payment-related communications
  4. Generate Link Analytics: To track and display analytics for your shortened links, including click counts, geographic location data, referrer sources, device types, and browser information
  5. Send Communications: To send transactional emails such as account verification, welcome messages, password reset instructions, payment confirmations, and security alerts. We also send service-related notifications including health check alerts for Pro users monitoring link performance, and marketing communications about product updates, new features, and promotions (which you may opt out of)
  6. Monitor Link Health: To check the status and availability of your destination URLs and notify you of any issues that could impact your affiliate revenue or link performance
  7. Improve and Optimize the Service: To analyze usage patterns, identify areas for improvement, develop new features, and enhance overall Service performance and user experience
  8. Prevent Fraud and Ensure Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities, abuse of the Service, security breaches, and violations of our Terms of Service
  9. Comply with Legal Obligations: To respond to legal requests, enforce our Terms of Service, protect our rights and property, and comply with applicable laws and regulations

Please note that analytics tracking is an integral part of our Service for registered users and cannot be disabled. If you do not wish to have your link analytics tracked, you may use our Service as a Guest User, which does not collect any data but provides links that expire after 7 days.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide, maintain, and improve our Service. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your device when you visit our website. Guest Users do not receive any cookies, as no authentication or tracking is performed for Guest links.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Authentication Cookies: We use a secure, HTTPS-only cookie to store a JSON Web Token (JWT) that contains only your user ID. This cookie is essential for maintaining your logged-in state and is verified on every request you make to our Service. Without this cookie, you cannot remain authenticated and use registered user features
  • Analytics Cookies: We use cookies to track usage patterns, analyze Service performance, and understand how users interact with our features. These cookies help us improve the Service and develop new functionality
  • Session Management Cookies: These cookies help us maintain your session state, remember your preferences, and provide a seamless user experience across multiple pages

Managing Cookies:

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, please note that disabling cookies will prevent you from using registered user features of our Service, as authentication cookies are essential for account functionality. If you disable cookies, you may still use our Service as a Guest User to create links that expire after 7 days.

For more information about managing cookies in your specific browser, please refer to your browser's help documentation.
